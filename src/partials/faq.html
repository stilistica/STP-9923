FAQ

  • Is Mystery Town free to play?

    Yes — it’s free with no ads, and optional in-app purchases for boosters or expansions.

  • What is the core gameplay?

    It’s a merge-based adventure — combine items to find clues, unlock areas, and advance the plot.

  • Do I need an internet connection?

    No. Offline play is supported, though certain updates and social features require internet.

  • How long is the story?

    Multiple chapters, regularly updated. Players can also revisit locations for new side missions.

  • Is this a casual or serious game?

    It blends both. The merging is intuitive, but the mystery runs deep.
