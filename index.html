Game Overview

Welcome to Mystery Town, a place where time stands still, secrets rot beneath the surface, and your only guide is a glowing trail of clues.
Join Aurora as she enters a town gripped by silence, shadows, and forgotten tragedies. A single match or merge might reveal truths no one dares speak aloud.

With every clue uncovered, you’ll rebuild, reconnect, and reveal the fate of her vanished uncle. Behind every object lies a fragment of the past — and perhaps, the final piece of a dangerous puzzle.

Solve. Merge. Reveal. The mystery won’t wait

Gameplay Features

Merge to Uncover

Drag and merge items to reveal advanced tools, hidden clues, and ancient symbols.

Clue-Driven Puzzles

Every successful merge moves the story forward — new hints appear as items evolve.

Town Restoration

Repair burned ruins, unlock haunted streets, and bring light to fog-covered neighborhoods.

Character Interactions

From cryptic strangers to old friends with secrets, each encounter may change the story’s course.

Dynamic Storyline

Multiple narrative threads with branching paths and moral choices.

Mystical Atmosphere

Rich audio effects, eerie background music, and foggy visuals immerse you fully.

Zero Ads, Pure Mystery

Dive deep without interruptions every clue counts.

Offline Progress

The town remembers your steps, even if you leave for a while.

FAQ

  • Is Mystery Town free to play?

    Yes — it’s free with no ads, and optional in-app purchases for boosters or expansions.

  • What is the core gameplay?

    It’s a merge-based adventure — combine items to find clues, unlock areas, and advance the plot.

  • Do I need an internet connection?

    No. Offline play is supported, though certain updates and social features require internet.

  • How long is the story?

    Multiple chapters, regularly updated. Players can also revisit locations for new side missions.

  • Is this a casual or serious game?

    It blends both. The merging is intuitive, but the mystery runs deep.
